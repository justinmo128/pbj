Egg Fried Rice

What is egg fried rice?

Fried rice. A staple of Asian cuisine, egg fried rice is one of the most popular variations of it. A wok is recommended, however it is not required. The name is quite self-explanatory.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Rice
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables (any)
  • Meat (any)
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oil

Instructions

These instructions are very unspecific because this is exactly how my mom cooks it.

  1. Throw all ingredients onto wok on high heat (no specific ratio).
  2. Stir well until rice looks fried enough and meat is cooked (personal preference).
  3. Done.
